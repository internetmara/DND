A World of Your Own

Your world is the setting for your campaign, the place where adventures happen. Even if you use an existing setting, such as the Forgotten Realms, it becomes yours as you set your adventures there, create characters to inhabit it and make changes to it over the course of your campaign. This generator is all about building your world and then creating a campaign to take place in it.
